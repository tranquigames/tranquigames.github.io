Privacy Policy — Sudokiller

Tranqui Games — Last updated: July 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes what information we collect through the video game Sudokiller (the "Application"), for what purpose, who we share it with, and what choices you have as a user. It should be read together with the Application's Terms of Use, which is a separate document.

01

Data controller

Tranqui Games is the developer and controller of the data described in this policy. You can contact us with any privacy-related question at: hellotranquigames@gmail.com.

02

What information we collect

Sudokiller uses the SDK of the external provider Talo (trytalo.com) for technical functions and aggregated usage statistics. Through this SDK we collect:

  • Device identifier (UUID): a random identifier generated and stored locally on your device, which lets us tell gameplay sessions apart. It is not linked to your name, email, or any other information that directly identifies you.
  • In-game interaction and progress data: menu events, use of in-game items, session times, and success/failure rates on levels.
  • Technical device metadata attached to each event: operating system, game version, window mode, and screen size.
  • IP address: as with any internet connection, your device transmits your IP address to Talo's servers when sending this information. We do not store it ourselves; it may be temporarily logged in Talo's infrastructure logs.

This information is sent automatically while you play, as part of the Application's technical operation. Talo's SDK specifically does not include leaderboards, player authentication, or syncing with your Steam or Google Play account: the data described in this section stays anonymous and does not identify you. Separately from Talo, if you're playing the Steam version, the Application may use native Steamworks features (such as achievements or cloud saves) that are tied to your Steam account; those features are handled directly by Valve/Steam under its own privacy policy (store.steampowered.com/privacy_agreement), not by Tranqui Games or Talo. We do not collect contact details (name, email, phone number), precise location, device contacts, or personal file content. If a future update changes what data we collect through Talo, we will update this policy before that change takes effect.

03

What we use this information for

  • Telling technical sessions apart and avoiding duplicate or inconsistent progress.
  • Generating aggregated usage statistics (for example, which levels are hardest) that help us improve the game's design.
  • Diagnosing technical errors and improving the Application's performance.

We do not use this information for advertising purposes, and we do not sell it to third parties.

04

Who we share information with

The technical data described in the previous section is processed by Talo (trytalo.com) as our infrastructure provider, solely to provide the analytics service described above. We recommend reviewing Talo's own privacy policy for more detail on how it handles data.

We do not share information with advertisers or data brokers, nor do we transfer it for purposes other than those described in this policy. Talo is the only third party we share the technical data described in this policy with; there are no advertising integrations or other analytics services in the Application. Native Steam features (such as achievements or Steam Cloud, if you use them on the Steam version) are handled directly between your account and Valve, without any involvement from us or Talo. On Android, the Application also runs on top of Google Play Services, Google's base service for Android apps; you can review Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

05

Children

Sudokiller is aimed at a general audience aged 13 and up. We do not knowingly request identifiable personal data from children under 13. If you're a parent or guardian and believe a child in your care used the Application and provided data without your consent, please write to us at hellotranquigames@gmail.com so we can look into deleting the corresponding technical identifier.

06

Data security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information processed by the Application. However, no data transmission or storage system is completely infallible, so we cannot guarantee absolute security against unauthorized access.

07

Your rights and how to exercise them

You can request from us at any time:

  • Information about what technical data is generated from your use of the Application.
  • Deletion of the identifier (UUID) associated with your device.

Since we don't maintain user accounts or named profiles, the most direct way to remove your identifier is to clear the Application's local data or uninstall it: on Steam, this means uninstalling the game from the client (and optionally deleting the local save folder); on Android, from the app's settings or by uninstalling it from your device. In either case, reinstalling the game generates a new UUID with no link to the previous one. If you'd rather we help you with this, write to us at hellotranquigames@gmail.com and we'll help however we're technically able to.

08

Data retention

Our policy is to keep technical data only for as long as it's needed for the purposes described in this policy. Tranqui Games does not maintain a separate database of its own: technical events are processed and stored directly on Talo's (trytalo.com) infrastructure, our analytics provider. If you uninstall the Application or clear its local data, we stop associating new information with that identifier and cannot recover it from your device. You can request deletion of the data already associated with your identifier at any time by writing to us at hellotranquigames@gmail.com, and we'll handle that request directly with Talo.

09

Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect technical, legal, or functional changes. We'll indicate the date of the last update at the top of the document. We recommend checking it periodically.

10

Contact

For any question about this Privacy Policy, you can write to us at hellotranquigames@gmail.com.
