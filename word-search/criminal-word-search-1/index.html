Word Search #1

Find the hidden words horizontally, vertically or diagonally. Three words from the list are missing: the culprit, the murder weapon, and the crime scene. Identify all three to solve the mystery.

Mark the letters as you find them

Deep in the forest, a crime has disturbed the silence of a secluded cabin. The clues are hidden in the grid, and it is up to you to discover who did it, which weapon they used, and where the crime took place.

    Click or tap individual letters to mark them, and click a word in the list to cross it out once you think you have found it. Click again to undo a mark. Your progress is saved automatically on this device.
