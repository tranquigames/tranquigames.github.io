Logic Grid Puzzle #1

The Five-Band Festival

Five bands are getting ready to perform at a festival. Find out which genre each one plays, how many years ago each one was formed, and how many members it has.

Clues

  1. The electronic band was formed after White Noise.
  2. The Fireflies has more than 6 members and does not play rock.
  3. Midnight was formed 7 years ago and plays neither pop nor jazz.
  4. The band with the most members is not The Comets and plays electronic music.
  5. The jazz band was formed one year before Saturn.
  6. Neither the band formed 3 years ago nor the band with 5 members is White Noise.
  7. Saturn plays punk and has 6 members.
  8. The band formed 2 years ago has more members than the pop band. The band with 8 members was formed 5 years before it.

Use the clues to correctly match the elements in each category. Click or tap a cell to mark a correct match, and right-click to place an X. Your progress is saved automatically on this device.
